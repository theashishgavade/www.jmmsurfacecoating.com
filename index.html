Who We Are

JMM Surface Coating

JMM Surface Coating has been actively operating since 2020 as a specialized Apply & Supply organization and was incorporated as a Private Limited entity in 2026.

The company delivers turnkey coating solutions covering surface preparation, application, quality control, safety compliance and project coordination — serving as a single accountable interface for clients.

The Group originated from JKP Paints and JKP Micro Minerals which operate independently with their own websites and manufacturing businesses. This website represents only JMM Surface Coating.

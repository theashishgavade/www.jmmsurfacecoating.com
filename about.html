Company Profile

Professional Apply & Supply Specialists

JMM Surface Coating is a professionally managed organization delivering integrated Apply & Supply solutions focused on professional surface preparation, paint application, and turnkey coating services.

The organization operates as a specialized execution arm evolved from a strong manufacturing and material science background developed over two decades through its parent companies.

Today, JMM Surface Coating serves as the single interface for clients seeking end-to-end coating solutions, ensuring accountability, execution excellence, safety compliance, and consistent quality delivery across all locations.
